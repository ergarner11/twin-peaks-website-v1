Welcome to Twin Peaks Veterinary Clinic!

We are an innovative, locally-owned practice offering veterinary care mainly to dogs and cats. We are saddened that financial barriers prevent far too many pets from receiving necessary health care. Our goal is to make quality care accessible to everyone, so we always charge only $20.00 for a full exam by one of our veterinarians. At Twin Peaks, we believe that people are able to make better decisions about the health of their pets when they are seen early.

We incorporate the latest developments in veterinary medicine into everything we do.We offer a broad spectrum of services and have an extensive in-house laboratory, digital X-ray, and top-of-the-line surgery suite which allow us to get answers faster and provide the highest quality care.

You will quickly come to love and trust our compassionate and highly-trained staff. We look forward to meeting you and your pet!
