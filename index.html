COVID-19

During these difficult times, Twin Peaks is excited that we are able to continue to serve your pets!

As we strive to keep our staff, our clients, and the community healthy and safe, we will introduce the following adjustments:

  • We will temporarily be closing at 6pm
  • In an effort to minimize person-to-person contact, clients will not enter our clinic. Instead, we will provide streamlined curbside services.
  • When you arrive at the clinic for your appointment, please remain in your car with your pet and give us a call at 970-663-0218. We will be right out! Your pet will be treated inside our clinic and be returned to you as soon as possible.

Rest assured that our doctors and technicians will take care of you and that your pet will receive the same exceptional services that we have always provided.

We appreciate your support as we work to serve you in new and exciting ways! We will continue to monitor the situation and follow the guidelines set forth by local and government agencies.

Please call if you have any questions.

Welcome to Twin Peaks Veterinary Clinic!

Home of the affordable Health Plans!

We are an innovative, locally-owned practice offering veterinary care mainly to dogs and cats. Our goal is to provide a way for pets to get the care they need by removing financial barriers for pet parents. Our Health Plans are designed to do exactly that!

Our Health Plans are different and flexible by nature. They are not packages of services, but rather packages of protection!
