Twin Peaks Health Plans- A New Standard in Veterinary Care!

Each Plan Includes Routine Preventative Care

At Twin Peaks, we understand that the high cost of veterinary care often prevents pets from getting basic wellness care. Because of that, each of our Health Plans covers wellness care, a spay/neuter, and a discount on Heartworm Prevention.

Get Dental Care Covered When You Need It

When it comes to dental care, you have options! In addition to our standard Health Plans with lower payments and no dental care coverage, we also have Dental Enhanced Health Plans which cover one dental cleaning and provide discounts for any other dental procedures that our veterinarians do in-clinic.

To top it off, we allow you to upgrade at any time to a Dental Enhanced Health Plan, which allows you to only pay for a Health Plan with Dental Coverage on years that you want to take advantage of that benefit.
